# A12. Discover 5 unique offline security tools

## Description
For this activity, I identified five offline security tools. Offline security tools are devices or physical tools that help protect people, property, or information without needing an internet connection.

## Selected tools

### 1. Fire extinguisher
![extinguisher](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/962d4c3d-1bac-4116-a34b-0e2d138ca7b8)

A fire extinguisher is a safety and security tool used to respond quickly to small fires. It helps reduce damage to property and lowers the risk to people in the area. Although it is mainly seen as a safety tool, it is also part of physical security because it protects assets and people from harm.

### 2. Door viewer or peephole
![peehole](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bbf3a3c2-1132-47dd-a512-2578a42e4766)

A door viewer allows someone inside a house or room to check who is outside before opening the door. It improves personal safety and helps prevent unauthorised or risky entry.

### 3. Portable document safe
![portable document safe](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/374fde61-4c98-430a-b5ca-bfcdfff05c84)
A portable document safe is used to store important papers such as passports, certificates, and legal documents. It protects them from theft, damage, or casual unauthorised access.


### 4. UV counterfeit money detector
![money detector](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/80834882-1d20-41db-ae56-e5138c713ff7)

A UV counterfeit money detector is an offline security tool used to check security features on banknotes. It helps identify fake currency by revealing marks or patterns that are not visible under normal light. This supports security by reducing fraud and protecting businesses from financial loss.

### 5. Steering wheel lock
![Anti-theft-locking-devices-for-your-car](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/01c685c1-1f77-46ab-95d9-fb6588971047)

A steering wheel lock is a physical anti-theft tool used in vehicles. It makes it harder for a thief to drive the vehicle away and also acts as a visible deterrent.


## Explanation
These tools work without relying on the internet or online services. They provide physical protection, improve safety, and help prevent theft, intrusion, or information exposure. Even simple offline tools can still play an important role in overall security.

## Reflection
This activity showed that security is not always digital. Many offline tools are still important in everyday life because they provide direct physical protection and reduce risks in practical situations.
