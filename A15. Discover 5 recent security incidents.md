# A15. Discover 5 recent security incidents

## Description
For this activity, I identified five recent security incidents reported in 2026. These cases show that security incidents can affect governments, companies, public platforms, and individual users. They also show that recent attacks involve different methods, including data theft, destructive attacks, and account compromise.

## Selected incidents

### 1. European Commission web platform cyberattack
In late March 2026, the European Commission reported a cyberattack affecting the cloud infrastructure supporting the Europa web platform. The Commission said the attack was contained and that its internal systems were not compromised, but initial findings suggested that data was extracted from affected websites.

Source: https://www.reuters.com/technology/eu-commission-web-platform-hit-by-cyber-attack-march-24-2026-03-27/

### 2. Kash Patel personal email breach
In March 2026, Reuters reported that Iran-linked hackers breached the personal Gmail account of FBI Director Kash Patel and published emails and personal photos online. The FBI confirmed the breach and said the exposed information was historical and not related to government matters.

Source: https://www.reuters.com/world/us/iran-linked-hackers-claim-breach-of-fbi-directors-personal-email-doj-official-2026-03-27/

### 3. Stryker cyberattack
In March 2026, U.S. medical device company Stryker reported system disruptions after an Iran-linked group claimed responsibility for a cyberattack. Reuters reported that staff saw the group’s logo on login screens and that data on some remote Windows-based devices had been wiped.

Source: https://www.reuters.com/technology/stryker-shares-fall-after-report-suspected-iran-linked-cyberattack-2026-03-11/

### 4. Crunchyroll data breach investigation
In March 2026, Crunchyroll said it was investigating a reported breach after hackers claimed to have stolen personal information linked to nearly 6.8 million users. The case shows how entertainment platforms can also become targets of large-scale data theft.

Source: https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/crunchyroll-probes-breach-after-hacker-steal-users-data-bleepingcomputer-reports-2026-03-23/

### 5. Cyberattack against a U.S. organisation’s endpoint management system
In March 2026, CISA issued an alert after a cyberattack against a U.S. organisation involving its endpoint management system. The alert focused on the need to harden management infrastructure because these systems can be highly valuable targets for attackers.

Source: https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/alerts/2026/03/18/cisa-urges-endpoint-management-system-hardening-after-cyberattack-against-us-organization


## Explanation
These incidents show that modern security incidents affect many different targets, including governments, healthcare-related companies, media platforms, and individuals. They also show that attacks can lead to data theft, service disruption, reputational damage, and exposure of private information.

## Reflection
This activity showed that security incidents are frequent and varied. It also showed the importance of incident response, system hardening, and fast communication after an attack is discovered.
