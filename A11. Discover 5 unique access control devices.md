# A11. Discover 5 unique access control devices

## Description
For this activity, I identified five different access control devices used to restrict entry to places, systems, or resources. Access control devices are designed to make sure only authorised people can gain access.

## Selected devices

### 1. Privacy screen protector
A privacy screen protector reduces the viewing angle of a phone or laptop screen. This makes it harder for people next to the user to read what is displayed. It is useful in public places such as libraries, cafes, and public transport.

### 2. Boom gate access system
A boom gate access system is used in car parks, gated communities, and restricted vehicle entry points. Access is usually controlled by a swipe card, remote control, licence plate recognition system, or security staff approval.


### 3. Shredding paper documents
Shredding printed documents before disposal helps protect personal information such as names, addresses, account numbers, or other private details. This reduces the risk of information being recovered from the rubbish.


### 4. RFID gate or turnstile
RFID gates or turnstiles are often used in train stations, office lobbies, and event venues. A user taps a card, ticket, or token, and the gate opens only if the credential is valid.


### 5. Intercom with remote unlock
An intercom system lets a person at the entrance request access. Someone inside the building can then verify the visitor and unlock the door remotely. This is commonly used in apartment buildings, offices, and gated properties.

## Evidence

## Explanation
These devices are used to control who can enter a location or use a system. They rely on different methods such as cards, biometrics, PINs, and remote approval. Although the devices are different, they all support the same goal of preventing unauthorised access.

## Reflection
This activity showed that access control devices are used in many everyday places. It also showed that access can be managed in different ways depending on the level of security needed.
